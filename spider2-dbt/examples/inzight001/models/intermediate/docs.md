The part of day is one of the following:

| part_of_day | description           |
| ----------- | --------------------- |
| night       | From 11 PM until 6 AM |
| morning     | From 6 AM until 12 PM |
| afternoon   | From 12 PM until 6 PM |
| evening     | From 6 PM until 11 PM |
