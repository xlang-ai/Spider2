## LN

LN(X)

**Description**

Computes the natural logarithm of X. Generates an error if X is less than or equal to zero.

X  |  LN(X)
---|---
1.0  |  0.0
` +inf ` |  ` +inf `
` X < 0 ` |  Error

**Return Data Type**

INPUT  |  ` INT64 ` |  ` NUMERIC ` |  ` BIGNUMERIC ` |  ` FLOAT64 `
---|---|---|---|---
OUTPUT  |  ` FLOAT64 ` |  ` NUMERIC ` |  ` BIGNUMERIC ` |  ` FLOAT64 `
