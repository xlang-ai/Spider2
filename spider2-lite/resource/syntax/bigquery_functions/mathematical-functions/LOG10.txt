## LOG10

LOG10(X)

**Description**

Similar to ` LOG ` , but computes logarithm to base 10.

X  |  LOG10(X)
---|---
100.0  |  2.0
` -inf ` |  ` NaN `
` +inf ` |  ` +inf `
X <= 0  |  Error

**Return Data Type**

INPUT  |  ` INT64 ` |  ` NUMERIC ` |  ` BIGNUMERIC ` |  ` FLOAT64 `
---|---|---|---|---
OUTPUT  |  ` FLOAT64 ` |  ` NUMERIC ` |  ` BIGNUMERIC ` |  ` FLOAT64 `
