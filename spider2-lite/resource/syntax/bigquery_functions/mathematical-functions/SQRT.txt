## SQRT

SQRT(X)

**Description**

Computes the square root of X. Generates an error if X is less than 0.

X  |  SQRT(X)
---|---
` 25.0 ` |  ` 5.0 `
` +inf ` |  ` +inf `
` X < 0 ` |  Error

**Return Data Type**

INPUT  |  ` INT64 ` |  ` NUMERIC ` |  ` BIGNUMERIC ` |  ` FLOAT64 `
---|---|---|---|---
OUTPUT  |  ` FLOAT64 ` |  ` NUMERIC ` |  ` BIGNUMERIC ` |  ` FLOAT64 `
